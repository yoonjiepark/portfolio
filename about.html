about me


hi! my name is yoonjie park, and i'm a human-centred product designer from seoul, south korea.

i'm a masters student at northwestern university studying engineering and design innovation. for my undergraduate degree, i studied manufacturing and design engineering and psychology. with my roots in journalism, i continue to write for articles for various publications in my free time.

with my hybrid background of technical engineering and user understanding, i hope to design products that solve real problems that real people have.

when i'm not working in the design studio, i enjoy exploring new places, challenging myself in escape rooms and spending time with my family, friends and my dog.
